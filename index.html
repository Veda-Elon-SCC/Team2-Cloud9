Nice Section

My Cooking

One of my interest outside the scool is cooking which I learned from my mom. When I lived in my country,Thailand, I liked to stay in the kitchen with my mom and helped her as assisstant; it made memories between our great relationships. The joy of cooking are that I can make food my family and I love to eat, and feel relax while I am cooking.

Since I have been living in the U.S. for four years, I always miss the original tastes of Thai cuisine, so I cook Thai food very often. I can say Thai cuisine is one of the most popular cuisines. Thai cuisine is based on special ingredients, such as herbs, and spices. It is not too hard to find basic Thai ingredients from Asian grocery in the U.S., but they are not fresh as buy them at the market in Thailand. I have fun with growing some Thai herbs in the summer, so I am proud of my activities in my garden. Cooking can be an enjoyable experience that is fun for good food, so it does not just a chore for me.

The keel-billed Toucan of Belize

Belize, an obsession

One of my favorite places to travel is Belize. Belize gained its independence from England in 1981, when it was known as British Honduras. It has a history full of intrigue, and was a destination for pirates in the 17th and 18th Centuries. It's a small English-speaking Central American country with only 348,000 people.

Belize is known for its coral reefs, jungles, wildlife, Mayan ruins and the Garifuna culture. I am a fan of the Belizean writer, Zee Edgell, and her succinct and evocative prose. Anyone wanting to get a better picture of Belizean life before/after independence should check out one of her books!
